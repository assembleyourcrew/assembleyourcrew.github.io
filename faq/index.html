What is Crew?

Crew is an Android application that shares your location with your Crew for a limited period. The location of your Crew Members is then overlaid on your phone screen.

How Does it Work?

Check out the Tutorial

What information does Crew Store about me?

Only your phone number is stored on Crew's Servers. This is to ensure that you can connect with your contacts. All other personal information like your name and location is shared with your Crew Members for the limited time that a Crew exists.

How do I find my Friends on Crew?

Crew uses the contacts that are already on your Phone so there is no need to manage them separately. Just add someone to your phone's contacts and if they have Crew installed they will be available.

Is Crew only on Android?

For now, Yes. If you would like to use crew on any other type of device please Let us Know.

Why does Crew need all those Permissions on my Android Device?

All the permissions that Crew uses are needed for the app to function correctly.

    Identity
  • read your own contact card - Crew gets your identity from your Contact Card
  • add or remove accounts - Crew creates an Account on your device
  • find accounts on the device - Crew needs to be able to find it's Account on your device
    Contacts
  • read your contacts - Crew uses your contacts to find your friends
  • modify your contacts - Crew marks which contacts of yours use Crew
    Location
  • precise location (GPS and network-based) - Crew needs to know where you are and shares your location with your Crew Memebers
    SMS
  • read your text messages (SMS or MMS) - Crew uses SMS' to validate your phone number
  • receive text messages (SMS) - Crew uses SMS' to validate your phone number
  • send SMS messages - Crew sends an SMS to validate your phone number
    Photos/Media/Files
  • modify or delete the contents of your USB storage - Used by the Google Services Library to cache data
  • read the contents of your USB storage - Used by the Google Services Library to cache data
    Camera
  • take pictures and videos - Crew overlays your crew Members locations onto your Camera
    Other
  • read sync statistics - Tells Crew whether your contacts are syncing with the crew server
  • receive data from Internet - Communicates with Crew's Servers
  • control vibration - Used for Notifications
  • use accounts on the device - Crew creates a local account on your device
  • read sync settings - Crew needs to know if it can sync
  • create accounts and set passwords - Crew creates a local account on your device
  • toggle sync on and off - Crew allows it's self to sync once installed
  • read Google service configuration - Crew uses GCM for messaging
  • prevent device from sleeping - Keep the device on while you're using Crew
  • view network connections - Makes sure you're connected
  • full network access - Communicates with Crew's Servers
Contact Us if we're missing anything.
